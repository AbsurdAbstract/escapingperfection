An Unlikely Encounter

My favorite form of art takes some people by surprise. It’s not music, or painting, or sculpture, it’s the art of games. Sometimes people ask me why, and how games can even be considered art. Ignoring the shallowness of the question, I usually answer that games are the art of laws. Music uses sound to convey thoughts, paintings uses paint, and games use rules. I actually find rules to be the most effective, and direct way to invoke thoughts or emotions in others.
